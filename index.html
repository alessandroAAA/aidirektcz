About Us

Where AI meets Life Sciences

AI-Direkt is a Czech-based AI company specializing in machine learning applications for the scientific and medical industries.

We bridge the gap between cutting-edge artificial intelligence research and real-world applications in pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and healthcare compliance. Our team combines deep expertise in ML engineering with domain knowledge in life sciences to deliver solutions that are both technically advanced and regulatory-compliant.

Founded with the vision of making AI accessible and reliable for the most critical applications, we partner with organizations across Europe to accelerate their scientific workflows while maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety.

Mission

Democratize AI for life sciences, making advanced ML tools accessible to researchers and compliance teams worldwide.

Vision

A future where every scientific discovery is accelerated by intelligent systems that understand the language of science.

Services

What we deliver

End-to-end AI solutions tailored for the pharmaceutical and life sciences industry.

Drug Discovery

ML-powered molecular screening, target identification, and lead optimization. We accelerate the pipeline from hit to candidate with predictive models trained on vast chemical libraries.

  • Virtual screening & docking
  • ADMET profiling

02

GMP Compliance

Intelligent systems for Good Manufacturing Practice compliance. Automated deviation detection, real-time process monitoring, and smart documentation workflows that reduce human error.

  • Automated batch record review
  • Deviation detection & CAPA
  • Process monitoring & control
  • Regulatory documentation AI
03

Predictive Analytics

  • Supply chain intelligence

Production-grade ML infrastructure designed for regulated environments. From model training to deployment, with full audit trails and validation frameworks.
